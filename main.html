Puri Jagannath


Puri is one of India’s most famous coastal pilgrimage towns — known for the ancient Shri Jagannath Temple, golden beaches and vibrant festivals. The sea breeze, local food and spiritual atmosphere make it a memorable holiday destination for pilgrims and tourists alike.

  1. Shri Jagannath Temple — The principal temple and the heart of Puri's cultural life.
  2. Puri Golden Beach — Famous for long walks, sunrises and beach festivals.
  3. Gundicha Temple — The temporary residence of the deities during the Rath Yatra.
  4. Chilika Lake (Satapada) — Asia's largest brackish water lagoon, noted for dolphins and birdlife.
  5. Konark Sun Temple — A UNESCO World Heritage Site (near Puri) famed for its stone chariot and carvings.
  • Mahaprasad (Abhada) — Sacred temple food cooked in earthen pots.
  • Khaja — Crispy layered sweet soaked in sugar syrup.
  • Chhena Poda — Caramelized baked cheese dessert made from chhena.
  • Puri Aloo Dum — A spicy, comforting breakfast combo.
  • Dahi Bara Aloo Dum — Soft dahi vadas paired with spicy aloo dum.
