Puri Jagannath


Puri Jagannath is one of India’s most sacred and scenic holiday destinations. Known for the ancient Jagannath Temple, beautiful beaches and rich culture, it attracts millions of visitors each year.

  1. Shri Jagannath Temple – Main attraction and holy site.
  2. Puri Golden Beach – Beautiful clean shoreline.
  3. Gundicha Temple – Important during Rath Yatra festival.
  4. Chilika Lake – Home to dolphins and migratory birds.
  5. Konark Sun Temple – UNESCO heritage site near Puri.
  • Mahaprasad – Temple’s sacred food.
  • Khaja – Crispy sweet snack.
  • Chhena Poda – Caramelized cheese dessert.
  • Puri Aloo Dum – Famous spicy breakfast.
  • Dahi Bara Aloo Dum – Popular street food.
